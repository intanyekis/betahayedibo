    The social media drama unfolded when Darius Jackson, the baby daddy of American actress Keke Palmer, faced backlash for publicly criticizing an outfit she wore to Usher’s show. A viral clip captured Keke Palmer being serenaded by Usher at his Las Vegas residency, where she was seen wearing a sheer black dress and dancing with the singer. Following this, Jackson publicly shamed Palmer for her outfit choice, quoting a tweet of the video and commenting, “It’s the outfit tho… you a mom.”

    Despite previously celebrating Palmer’s body in his Instagram Stories, fans swiftly condemned Jackson’s apparent shaming of her. Jackson further defended his opinion in a later tweet, expressing his belief in standards and morals regarding family representation.

    Although Palmer has not directly addressed her baby daddy’s comments, it was noticed that the couple no longer follow each other on Instagram. The pair welcomed their son, Leodis, in February, with Palmer sharing the joyous news on Instagram. While their relationship has mostly been kept out of the public eye, they had made it Instagram official in August of the previous year.
