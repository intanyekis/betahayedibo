    James Cameron is famous for directing the sci-fi blockbuster Avatar as well as the classic 1997 Titanic. He is currently the 3rd richest movie director in the world. His movie Avatar had been the highest-grossing movie at the box office since 2009, before it was overtaken by Avengers: Endgame, in 2019.

    It remains the biggest grossing movie at the international box office ($2.029 billion). James Cameron currently has a net worth of $800 million. So, without further ado, here are 10 insanely expensive things the movie director has bought.Well, maybe not all of them (the watch was a gift).

    A Mansion

    Cameron owns a lavish home where he and his wife, Suzy Amis live with their four children, Josephine, Quinn, Claire, and Elizabeth. The mansion, which is located in Malibu, California, covers an area of more than 8,000 square feet with six bedrooms, seven bathrooms, a guest house, a gourmet kitchen, an interior garden, a tennis court, two swimming pools, courtyard, and an indoor cinema.

    RELATED: 10 Of Camila Cabello’s Most Expensive Outfits

    Cameron also has a built-in home office within his mansion which includes a private studio for his work. The mansion is said to have cost the movie director $5 million. In 2007 he listed the house for over $24 million.

    A Winery - $2.9 Million

    The director owns a private winery in the Comox Valley, British Columbia. The winery covers a total of 84 acres. The establishment was the winner of the Northwest Wine Summit in Oregon, the All Canadian Wine Championships and 27 medals at the International Wine Challenge in London. Understandably named Cameron Winery they produce wine using sustainable methods. The winery started some years ago and was recently bought by James Cameron for $2.9 million. The winery whose location was also named a PGA Tour Canada qualifying school site is rumored to be a home base for him. He has been producing his wine since 1984.

    A Home - $20 Million

    Besides his Malibu mansion, Cameron boasts of a home in New Zealand which is considered an away home for him, though he had plans of moving there with his family for an indefinite amount of time. The property is divided into two, covering a total of over 1,000 acres, featuring several bedrooms, bathrooms, a kitchen, home offices, and living rooms. The total of this massive property cost the movie director $20 million and is located near Wellington. Though a lot of people, especially New Zealanders tend to worry concerning his ability to inhabit this massive property, Cameron is not wavered by any of those.

    Submarines - $3 Million

    James Cameron owns a fleet of Triton submarines that he bought when he was shooting the movie Titanic. They were used to explore the deep ocean to recover artifacts from the wreckage of the real Titanic, and possibly discover something new. Though he used a different power diver to explore the deepest part of the ocean, the Mariana Trench, all his underwater shots were aided by his Triton submarines. Each submarine cost him an average of $3 million. The movie director embarked on a mission to the point in the pacific called the ‘Challenger Deep’ in 2012. Tritons can dive an astounding 36,000 feet and can carry up to three occupants.

    A Yacht - $17-$20 Million

    Cameron owns an Alucia 2 yacht. James Cameron is not only a record-breaking movie director but has also become popular in ocean exploration and globetrotting, so he bought an Alucia 2 yacht. It was built in 2010 and he equipped the water mover with every scientific technology he would require for his ocean explorations and personal vacations.

    RELATED: The 10 Most Expensive Films Helena Bonham Carter Starred In (& How Much They Made)

    The equipment includes an array of advanced camera systems for capturing the deep blue in great detail. It was for shooting the BBC’s Blue Planet. The yacht also has a helipad and can launch submersibles, tow sonar machines and drag nets. The Alucia 2 costs between $17 million to $20 million.

    A Sports Car - $36,000-$55,000

    Cars are quite expensive, what’s more, they become even more so when they are sports cars and a lot of celebrities are fond of purchasing insanely expensive cars. We all know of cars that cost a fortune, with the Bugatti and Ferrari being just a few. Cameron’s Corvette C6 Convertible isn’t about to be left behind in the expense discussion. With a $2,000 optional folding roof, this powerhouse can range between $36,000 to $55,000 depending on the additional props you chose. The 2020 model, mid-Engine Corvette Stingray costs as much as $60,000. The Corvette C6 convertible is the 6th generation of Corvette cars, with specified models being ZR1 and 427 Convertible.

    Harley Davidson - $19,000-$28,000

    James owns three Harley’s. His these motorcycles make use of the 45-degree air cooled V-Twins, and can reach a mind-blowing speed. To let you know just how fast these machines can go, the record Harley Davidson top speed was 332.15 mph and has been unbroken for the past 16 years. Quite a frightening speed to move on a motorbike. One of his Harleys was featured in the movie Terminator 2: Judgment Day!Harley Davisons can cost between $19,000 to a little over $28,000. Harley’s model Comic Starship is the 2nd most expensive motorcycle in the world. Even multi-millionaires deserve to feel the wind in their hair.

    A Watch - $13,000

    James Cameron’s Rolex Deepsea watch was valued at $13,000. In the 21st century, Rolex (being one of the most expensive wristwatch brands) has not been so adventurous when it comes to product reputation and development and only a few people get to have their own branded Rolex. James Cameron is one of those few. Made in honor of him and his new film, it was released in 2014 with a customized dial that starts from blue and goes down to black in a gradient to commemorate a special event where the movie director went to the deepest part of the deep blue.

    Another Motorcycle!

    James obviously has a love for motorcycles. Maybe it’s the danger involved in the riding of these machines he enjoys (after all he is known for directing famous danger-filled movies), whatever it is we will never know until we ask him. The Ducati 848 EVO owned by James Cameron, weighs about 60 pounds with a speed of up to 356km/h (159 mph). The Ducati 848 EVO is one of the fastest motorcycles in the world. The Ducati also boasts another model, the Ducati 1098 that can run at a speed of 169 mph (271 km/h) and is the 10th fastest in the world.

    New Zealand Farms

    Apart from his two other properties which were listed above, Cameron has spent millions expanding his estate in New Zealand. The dairy farm covers around 250 hectares and was sold to the movie director by Heather and Rob McCreary. James also holds New Zealand residency and is reported to have plans of working on the Avatar sequels soon (using the property of course). The director is a vegan and an activist at that. He has turned the dairy into a farm for crops, campaigning for moving away from meat and planting vegetables. He believes that moving to crops can help New Zealand’s dairy industry as the country moves to a low-emissions country.
