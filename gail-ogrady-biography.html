    Who is Gail O’Grady?

    Gail O’Grady is an American actress and a film producer. She is well known for her portrayal of Donna Abandando in the mystery crime drama “NYPD Blue”, created by Steven Bochco and David Milch, and which starred Dennis Franz, Jimmy Smits, and Gordon Clapp.

    Grady is also familiar with her lead roles in several television movies. She has been selected for a Primetime Emmy Award three times.

    Gail O’Grady- Professional Life, Career

    Gail O’Grady started her career as a model for Montgomery Ward by making several commercials. She took part in the neighborhood for whom Michael J. Fox’s character chased down a Diet Pepsi.

    In time, she began making guest appearances, often as a villain. She made her big-screen debut in the 1988 romantic comedy and later she was cast in a recurring role on the ABC drama China Beach, opposite Dana Delany.

    She played her first major part as Donna Abandando on the ABC police drama NYPD Blue. From 1993 to 1996, Grady played the big-haired squad secretary, and for each year achieve an Emmy Award nomination for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Drama Series.

    Along with the cast, she won Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by an Ensemble in a Drama Series in 1995. Gail left the series in 1996 and made a pilot for her sitcom, The Gail O’Grady Project, but the show was not picked up by any of the networks.

    From 2002 to 2005, Gail O’Grady acted as Helen Pryor in the NBC television series American Dreams, which depicted an all-American family living in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, during the 1960s. The series was canceled after three seasons.

    In fall 2005, Grady was the lead actress in the ABC comedy series Hot Properties. The series was canceled after 13 episodes.

    In 2006 she starred in Two and a Half Men, and in 2007 had a recurring role on Boston Legal as Gloria Weldon, a judge in a personal relationship with attorney Alan Shore. Also in 2007, she perform in the Hallmark Channel movie All I Want for Christmas.

    Gail O’Grady had a series of regular characters on the short-lived CW series Hidden Palms as Karen Hardy in 2007, and on Hellcats, playing Wanda Perkins, the mother of the main character from 2010 to 2011.

    In 2008 she had a recurring role in the ABC series Desperate Housewives as Anne Schilling, a woman who is having an affair with the character Porter Scavo. She also has made appearances on Monk, CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, The Mentalist, Law & Order: Special Victims Unit, Drop Dead Diva, Hawaii Five-0, and Castle.

    Also, Gail O’Grady was assigned to a major recurring role as Conrad’s first wife on ABC primetime soap opera Revenge, opposite Madeleine Stowe.
