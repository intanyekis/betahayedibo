    07 Jun 2024, 00:00

    WHOOPS! ESPN recruiting analyst Gerry Hamilton directed his followers to a different kind of national pastime on Wednesday, Jan. 21. In an attempt to offer an update on ESPN’s 29th ranked football recruit in the country, Roquan Smith, Hamilton accidentally tweeted out a link to PornHub.

    “#ESPN300 No. 29 Roquan Smith schedules Texas A&M OV Jan. 23. #UCLA Jan. 30. Already visited #UGA & #Michigan. #Gigem pornhub.com/view_video.php…” the tweet read, letting fans know about Smith’s scheduled official visits as the Feb. 4 signing day draws near. Hamilton seemingly meant to link to the Montezuma, Georgia player’s highlight video, but clearly posted the wrong link.

    The analyst then corrected his error and tweeted instead: “#ESPN300 No. 29 Roquan Smith schedules OV to Texas A&M Jan. 23. #UCLA Jan. 30. Already OV to #UGA & #Michigan. #Gigem http://espn.go.com/college-sports/football/recruiting/player/_/id/177402/roquan-smith…”
