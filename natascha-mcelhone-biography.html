    Who is Natascha McElhone?

    Natascha McElhone is an English actress.

    She is best known for her roles in American films.

    She started acting in the theater before making her television debut in the series Ruth Rendell Mysteries in 1990. Her birth name is Natasha Abigail Taylor.

    Her other names are Natasha McElhone and Natascha Taylor.

    She is also best known for the movie, “The Truman Show”.

    What are the birth facts of Natascha McElhone?

    Natascha was born on December 14, 1969, in Walton On Thames, Surrey, England.

    Her nationality is British. Her ethnicity is mixed(English, Irish).

    She is the daughter of Noreen (mother) and Mike Taylor (father).

    Her parents were separated when she was two years old.

    After separating from her parents her mother along with her and her brothers moved to Brighton and married Roy Greenslade who is a columnist and also a journalist.

    Education History

    From 6 years old to 12 years old she had taken a lesson in Irish Dancing and graduated from the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art.

    She had attended St. Mar’s Hall School in Brighton.

    Professional Life and Career

    Natascha started her career in the theater, starring in productions such as Richard III and The Count Of Monte Cristo.

    Natascha had done many films as well as television series.

    She made her debut on television in 1991 on BBC’s Bergerac.

    Natascha had also seen in an episode of Absolutely Fabulous in 1992. She had acted in many films including The Devil’s Own in 1997, The Truman Show with Jim Carrey in 1998, Ronin in 1998, Contaminated Men in 2000, Solaris in 2002, Ladies in Lavender in 2001, Big Nothing in 2006, Blessed in 2008 and The Kid in 2010.

    Natascha co-starred in the NBC miniseries named Revelations in 2005 with an actor named Bill Pullman. She had given her voice in a video game called Castlevania in 2010.
