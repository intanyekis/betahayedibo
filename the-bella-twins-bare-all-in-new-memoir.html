    The Bella Twins Bare All In New Memoir

    Twins Nikki and Brie Bella may no longer work their magic in the wrestling ring, but these ladies are still living life to the fullest. Both ladies are currently pregnant, as we recently reported on The Things, and in true twin fashion, their due dates are less than 2 weeks apart.

    19 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Twins Nikki and Brie Bella may no longer work their magic in the wrestling ring, but these ladies are still living life to the fullest.

    Both ladies are currently pregnant, as we recently reported on The Things, and in true twin fashion, their due dates are less than 2 weeks apart.

    Gearing up to motherhood isn’t the only thing these 36-year-olds are up to these days...

    From WWE To Writing

    Out on May 5, their joint memoir titled Incomparable will reveal the twins’ journey thus far, filled with “loss, abuse, and plenty of rough times,” as per a press release announcing the book, as reported by Page Six.

    On Instagram, Nikki wrote, “In this memoir we share stories that we never have told before and how we became the heroes of our own stories.”

    There are no “sneak peeks” into what she’s referring to at this point, but the tease is sure to get fans curious.

    Related: 18 Pics Showing How Much Nikki Bella Has Changed Since Starting In WWE

    The Pair Gets Personal

    We’ve seen so much of the twins’ lives on reality TV, but there’s much more to their story they’re finally ready to share with the world.

    Brie said, “The bumps in the road make us stronger and the parts of us that we feel the weakest mold us into an unstoppable force.”

    So Many Memoirs, So Little Time

    As we recently reported on The Things, Jessica Simpson also came out with her memoir, Open Book which is full of secrets and stories that were shocking to her fans.

    Will the Bella’s memoir be as juicy? We’ll have to wait until May to find out.
