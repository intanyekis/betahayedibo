    The Vampire Diaries actress, Kat Graham is taken!

    Congratulations are in order to newly engaged couple Kat Graham and Darren Genet. The 32-years-old actress has been dating a producer and director since 2017. They met on the set of “The Vampire Diaries”.

    A source confirmed to PEOPLE magazine that Genet proposed to the actress during a vacation in Mexico for his birthday a few weeks ago. Graham’s rep confirmed the news to the outlet,

    “Kat is madly in love and couldn’t be happier,”

    Darren shared photos of his birthday celebration with his fiancee hugging under a palm tree and sharing a kiss inside a villa. He also captioned the post,

    “Blissed out and incredibly grateful for this birthday weekend spent at @thehouseofaia with such amazing people. Life changing! Thanks to my love @katgraham for bringing me here for such a profound experience.”

    As soon as he drop the post, Kat commented,

    “Love you baby!!!!!!!!!!”

    Sneak peek at their relationship

    The two have kept their relationship mostly private and have rarely been snapped together out and about or on red carpets. Also, the lovebirds hardly shared glimpses of their romance on social media platforms.

    They were seen together last time in December to honor jewelry designer Lorraine Schwartz at the American Friends of the Israel Philharmonic Orchestra.

    The same month, Kat took Instagram to share her holiday celebration with Darren. She shared a photo of the two sharing a kiss in front of a decorated Christmas tree and getting cozy in front of a life-size blue menorah. She captioned the post,

    “Merry Christmas everyone!!!!! Wishing you the most beautiful Christmas, Hanukkah, Kwanzaa! However you celebrate, we hope your days are filled with love and happiness.”
