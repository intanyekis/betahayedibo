    Big Brother Fans Are Praising Claire For Leaving $750,000 Behind So The Cookout Alliance Can Advance

    On Wednesday night's episode of Big Brother, Claire Rehfuss ultimately sacrificed her chance at three-quarters of a million dollars for the bigger picture here. Fans watched in awe as Claire let her best friend and ally put her on the block to be sent to the jury without a complaint.

    20 Jul 2024, 00:00

    On Wednesday night's episode of Big Brother, Claire Rehfuss ultimately sacrificed her chance at three-quarters of a million dollars for the bigger picture here. Fans watched in awe as Claire let her best friend and ally put her on the block to be sent to the jury without a complaint.

    Tiffany was forced to nominate her closest ally since she was not a part of The Cookout alliance which consists of three men and three women of color. The Cookout has been working together secretly since day one, but this still wasn't an easy decision for the HOH.

    The Cookout was formed by Tiffany, and she recruited Kyland, Xavier, Derek F., Azah, and Hannah. Their goal was very clear: they wanted to see a Black person walking away with that $750,000 grand prize. On top of that, they wanted all final six contestants to be people of color.

    In an emotional scene, Tiffany let Claire know that she had to put her on the block because she needed to ensure a Black winner this season.

    “So many people won’t get it,” Claire said in her diary room video after learning of her fate. “But I get it. And that’s why I can’t fight her on this.”
