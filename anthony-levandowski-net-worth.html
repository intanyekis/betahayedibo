    After his departure from Google, Anthony Levandowski ventured into the world of autonomous vehicles, co-founding the company Otto. This endeavor gained significant attention and was later acquired by Uber, with Levandowski playing a pivotal role in Uber’s driverless car division.

    In addition to Otto, Levandowski founded Pronto, a company dedicated to advancing self-driving technology for specialty environments.     The Consequences of Bankruptcy

    Bankruptcy is a legal process that offers individuals and businesses the opportunity to eliminate or repay their debts under the protection of the court. While it provides relief from overwhelming financial burdens, it also comes with significant consequences.

    Additionally, bankruptcy may require the liquidation of assets to repay creditors. This can result in the loss of valuable possessions, further diminishing an individual’s financial standing.
