    Annie Richardson Obituary, Death – In a tragic turn of events, one of the victims of the Louisiana Tech University stabbings has succumbed to her injuries, as confirmed by Ruston Mayor Ronny Walker. The victim, identified as Annie Richardson, was wounded during the disturbing incident at the Lambright Sports and Wellness Center. Louisiana Tech University President Les Guice expressed his condolences, extending his prayers to Annie’s husband, Peter, and the entire Richardson family.

    President Guice remarked, “My prayers are with Annie’s husband Peter, the entire Richardson family, and all who called Annie a friend.” He acknowledged the profound impact this incident would have on the Tech family, emphasizing the significance of the supportive community during this challenging time. The university president recognized the collective grief that would linger within the community as they cope with the loss.

    Tom Soto, the interim director of university communications for Louisiana Tech, also released a statement mourning the tragic passing of Annie Richardson. Soto acknowledged the pain felt by the entire Louisiana Tech community, reflecting on the severity of the incident that unfolded at the Lambright Sports and Wellness Center. In a somber update, it was revealed that Jacoby Johnson, the suspect in the stabbings, is now facing an additional charge of second-degree murder in connection with Annie Richardson’s death.

    As the university grapples with the aftermath of this heartbreaking incident, the statements from President Guice and Tom Soto highlight the unity and support within the Louisiana Tech community. The loss of Annie Richardson serves as a stark reminder of the fragility of life and the profound impact such events can have on a close-knit academic community.
