    Over the years, Netflix has become host to a wide range of popular TV shows, reality shows, and movies. Some of these include Squid Game, Love Is Blind, Selling Sunset, and of course, Too Hot To Handle. Since Too Hot To Handle first aired in April 2020, it quickly became a fan favorite. Each week, fans tuned in with much anticipation to watch the drama unfold. However, what was the catch? What could possibly be so difficult about spending some time in the sun with a bunch of attractive singles?

    RELATED: Here's How Francesca Farago Reconnected With Harry Jowsey Post-Breakup

    It turned out that all the participants on the show had to follow a strict set of rules. They must refrain from engaging in any form of physical intimacy, including kissing, or risk losing both the cash prize and their spot on their show. This made the show an instant talking point, and quickly the contestants on the show became highly popular on social media. However, fans naturally developed favorites, including the likes of Harry Jowsey, and his partner at the time, Francesca Farago. Now, many fans have been left wondering who he is dating since leaving the show.

    Harry's First Public Relationship Was With Francesca Farago

    After leaving the hit reality show, Harry Jowsey continued his public relationship with Francesca Farago, with many fans 'shipping' the loved-up pair.

    Fans got an inside glimpse of their relationship on social media during this time, and the pair even went on to visit each other in both Australia and Vancouver.

    They continued to date on and off for the next two years after the show aired.

    However, despite being much loved by fans, the couple eventually parted ways in 2020.

    The pair reportedly broke up due to the long distance, which made it challenging for the two to see each other as they lived on opposite sides of the world.

    Since then, Jowsey has been spotted with several girls, however, none of them seemed to develop into serious relationships. Fans even speculated at one point that he and fellow YouTuber Tana Mongeau could have something going on behind closed doors.

    RELATED: Harry Jowsey Gives Starbucks Barista $2k In Moving TikTok Video

    However, now fans have noticed a potentially more serious candidate in Jowsey's dating life, with media coverage showing the pair getting closer as more than just friends. But who exactly is this mystery woman that Jowsey appears to be dating?

    Harry Is On And Off With Georgia Hassarati

    According to Reality Titbit, Harry Jowsey is currently dating Georgia Hassarati, after the two briefly split for a short period. Their romance officially began back in April 2022, after Hassarati left Season 3 of Too Hot To Handle as a single woman.

    Throughout this time period, the loved-up couple often shared romantic snaps together via social media, which fans seemed to adore.

    This is also how fans discovered that the pair were once again back together, after Hassarati posted a TikTok in September 2022 captioned, "When captain dramatic wants to announce we are broken up after one fight” alongside a video of the two fondly hugging one another.

    However, neither of the reality stars has made an official statement on their official relationship status, which has left some fans puzzled.

    Jowsey has also previously opened up about the difficulties the two have faced as a couple.

    In an exclusive interview with Page Six, Jowsey detailed how the distance between them has been challenging, saying, "Thank God I’m leaving tomorrow to go see her, but the distance … it’s been very testing on our relationship because we don’t communicate in the same frequency if that makes sense. Like, she needs a couple of days to cool off things, and I need to get through whatever the issue is like right away so it can stop being serious."

    Since then, Hassarati has gone on to become a social media influencer and model, which is presumably how she earns much of her income.

    They Met On His Podcast, Tap In With Harry Jowsey

    Fans seem to love both Georgia and Harry together. However, despite having a very public relationship, many fans have been left wondering exactly how the pair met.

    It turns out that Jowsey and Hassarati both met for the first time on Harry's YouTube podcast Tap In With Harry Jowsey. Many viewers of the show noted that the pair seemed to have had undeniable chemistry, leaving many with hope that they would wind up together.

    Jowsey also hasn't been shy to share some of his best dating tips with his fans, adding that he likes to show people that he's 'the best time' and 'fun', as well as opting for cute yet creative names when greeting someone.

    He has also shared that he likes to ask lots of questions when he is on a date with a girl, as it allows him to get to know them as good as he possibly can.
